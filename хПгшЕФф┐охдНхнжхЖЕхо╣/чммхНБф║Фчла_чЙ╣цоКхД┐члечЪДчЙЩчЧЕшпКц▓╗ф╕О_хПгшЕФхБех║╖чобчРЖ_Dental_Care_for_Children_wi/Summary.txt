
It has been recognized that providing both primary and comprehensive preventive and therapeutic oral health care to children with special health care needs is an integral part of the pediatric dentistry in China. The dental condition of children with special health care needs is not optimistic. The pediatric dentists should value the unique oral care needs of each person and ensure maximal healt! attainment for all, regardless of developmental disability or other special health care needs. In this chapter, the managements of oral health care needs for children with special health care needs are discussed. A pediatric dentist should be familiar with the special health care needs of a special child and with parents' concerns. The dental management of the child can be gratifying.
