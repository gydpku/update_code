
颞下颌关节紊乱病(temporomandibular disorders):It is the name given to a group of symptoms that cause pain in the head, face, and jaw. The symptoms include headaches, soreness in the chewing muscles, abnormality in mandibular movement, and clicking or stiffness of the joints. They often have psychological as well as physical causes.

骨关节病 (osteoarthrosis): Which is also known as osteoarthritis or degenerative joint disease ( DJD ), is a progressive degenerative disorder of the joints caused by gradual loss of cartilage and results in the development of bony spurs and cysts at the margins of the joints.

类风湿关节炎 (rheumatic arthritis): A chronic systemic disease primarily of the joints, usually polyarticular, marked by inflammatory changes in the synovial membranes and articular structures and by atrophy and rarefaction of the bones. In late stages, deformity and ankylosis develop.

滑膜软骨瘤病(synovial chondromatosis):Synovial chondromatosis is a benign nodular cartilaginous proliferation arising in the synovium of joints, bursae or tendon sheaths.

弥漫型腱鞘巨细胞瘤 (diffuse-type giant cell tumour of tendon sheath): Diffuse-type giant cell tumour is a destructive proliferation of synovial-like mononuclear cells, admixed with multinucleate giant cells, foam cells, siderophages and inflammatory cells. The extra-articular form is defined by the presence of an infiltrative soft tissue mass, with or without involvement of the adjacent joint.
