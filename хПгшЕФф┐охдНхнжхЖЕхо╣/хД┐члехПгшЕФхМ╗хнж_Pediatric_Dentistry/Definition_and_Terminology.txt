
1. 现病史 (history of present illness): An account obtained during the interview with the patient of the onset, duration, and character of the present illness, as well as of any acts or factors that aggravate or ameliorate the symptoms.

2. 临床检查 (clinical examination): The process by which a doctor investigates the body of a patient for signs of disease.

3. 牙科治疗计划(dental treatment plan):A schedule of procedures and appointments designed to restore, step by step, a patient's oral health.

4. 病历 ( patient record ) : Provides all privileged parties with the history and details of patient assessment and communications between dentist and patient, as well as specific treatment recommendations, alternatives, risks, and care provided.

(赵玉鸣 李 刚)
