
生长期 Development It occurs in a stepwise fashion for both primary dentition and permanent dentition, which is also called odontogenesis and takes place in many stages.

钙化期 Calcification

It is one of the developmental stages of teeth. During this stage, the hard tissues including dentin, enamel and cementum are formed.

萌出期 Eruption

A term used to denote the tooth's emergence through the gingiva, which becomes more completely defined to mean continuous tooth movement from the dental bud to occlusal contact.
