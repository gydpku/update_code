
Pain control is very important for pediatric dental treatment. Painful dental experience during childhood will have a lifelong impact on children's dental attitude. One of the effective methods for pain control is local anesthesia. Understanding the features and methods of pediatric intraoral local anesthesia are important for getting good anesthetic effect and guaranteeing the process of dental treatment. The most fearful dental treatment for children is tooth extraction. However sometimes teeth have to be extracted to ensure normal development and eruption of permanent successors. This chapter introduces to fundamental principles of pediatric dental surgery, indications, methods and skills of pediatric tooth extraction.
