
This section mainly describes the commonly used materials and equipment of the implant laboratory, which is the key factor of improving the efficiency and the quality of implant prosthesis products. Machining accuracy is the first element of guaranteeing the long term success of any prosthesis. Technicians involved with the oral implantology technique work should not only be familiar with all the components used for prosthetic treatment, grasp the knowledge and skills of conventional denture technology, but also need to understand the disciplines related to oral implantology.
