
1. 系统性疾病 (systemic disease): It is one that affects a number of organs and tissues, or affects the body as a whole.

2. 遗传病 ( genetic disease ): It is an illness caused by abnormalities in genes or chromosomes, especially a condition that is present from before birth.

3. 遗传咨询 (genetic counseling): It is the process by which patients or relatives, at risk of an inherited disorder, are advised of the consequences and nature of the disorder, the probability of developing or transmitting it, and the options open to them in management and family planning.

(赵玉鸣 杨 媛)
