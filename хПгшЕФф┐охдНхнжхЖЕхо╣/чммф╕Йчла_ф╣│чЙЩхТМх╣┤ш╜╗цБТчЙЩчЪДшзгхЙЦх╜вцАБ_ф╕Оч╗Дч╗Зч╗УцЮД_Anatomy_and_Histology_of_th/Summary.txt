
This chapter introduces the anatomic morphology of each primary tooth in the aspects of tooth shape, size and shape of pulp chamber, root and root canal. Compared to the roots of permanen teeth, the roots of primary teeth have their own characteristics and undergo physiological resorption During root resorption, the pulp tissue of primary teeth exhibits corresponding changes. The differences between primary teeth and permanent teeth, the significance of primary teeth and the feature of young permanent teeth are also addressed.
